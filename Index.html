About Dr. B. Krishna Murthy

Dr. B. Krishna Murthy is an internationally recognized artist, educator, and mentor from Mahabubabad, Telangana, India. With a lifelong passion for painting and creative expression, he has dedicated more than three decades to promoting art, inspiring young artists, and preserving cultural values through his work.

Beginning his artistic journey at an early age, Dr. Murthy successfully completed the Drawing Lower Grade and Higher Grade examinations, laying the foundation for a remarkable artistic career.

Alongside his artistic achievements, he pursued higher education in English Literature, B.Ed., and M.Ed., eventually serving as a Government Teacher while continuing his passion for visual arts.

His artwork, created primarily with acrylic and oil mediums, beautifully reflects social awareness, human emotions, spirituality, and India's rich cultural heritage.

Today, with more than 545 original theme-based paintings, Dr. B. Krishna Murthy continues to inspire audiences across India and around the world through his creativity, dedication, and commitment to excellence.
